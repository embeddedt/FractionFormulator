Welcome to the Fraction Formulator! Today you're going to build various fractions.

You can either use the arrows or type in a number for the numerator and denominator.

Your fraction will then be displayed in the form of a pizza and a divided rectangle.

You can play for as little or as long as you want. There are no time restrictions.

You're not allowed to make improper fractions (where the top number, or numerator is larger than the bottom number, or denominator).
